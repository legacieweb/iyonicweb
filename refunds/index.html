Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2025 | Last Updated: 1/1/2025

At Iyonicorp, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality ecommerce and service websites to our clients. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which you may be eligible for a refund for purchases made on iyonicorp.com.

1. General Refund Policy

Due to the custom and digital nature of our ecommerce and service website solutions, refunds are generally not offered once work has commenced or a product has been delivered, except under specific circumstances described below.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • The delivered website or service does not match the agreed-upon specifications and our support team cannot resolve the issue
  • The website or service is completely non-functional due to our error
  • Duplicate payments or accidental double orders
  • You request a refund within 7 days of purchase and before any custom work has started

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • You changed your mind after work has started or after delivery
  • The website or service does not fit your specific project after delivery
  • You modified the delivered website or service, causing it to break
  • You failed to review the project scope or product description before purchasing

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. 1. Email us at iyonicorp@gmail.com
  2. 2. Provide your Order ID, email, and a detailed reason for your refund request
  3. 3. Allow 3-5 business days for our team to review your request and respond
  4. 4. If approved, refunds will be processed via your original payment method (PayPal or Paystack)

5. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear in your account. If you experience delays, please contact your bank or payment provider.

6. Chargebacks & Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute without contacting us first, your account may be restricted or permanently banned from Iyonicorp services.
